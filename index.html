The Tafsir of Surat An-Nazi`at (Chapter - 79) Which was revealed in Makkah

Swearing by Five Characteristics that the Day of Judgement will occur

وَالنَّـزِعَـتِ غَرْقاً

(By those who pull out, drowning). Ibn Mas`ud, Ibn `Abbas, Masruq, Sa`id bin Jubayr, Abu Salih, Abu Ad-Duha and As-Suddi all said: "These are the angels who remove the souls from the Children of Adam." Among them are those whose souls are removed by the angels with difficulty, as if he is being drowned during its removal.

وَالنَّـشِطَـتِ نَشْطاً

(By those who free briskly.) There are those people whose souls the angels remove with ease, as if they were unraveling him (i.e., his soul from him) due to their briskness. This is the meaning of Allah's statement. This has been mentioned by Ibn `Abbas.

وَالسَّـبِحَـتِ سَبْحاً

(And by the swimmers, swimming.) Ibn Mas`ud said, "They are the angels." Similar statements have been reported from `Ali, Mujahid, Sa`id bin Jubayr, and Abu Salih.

فَالسَّـبِقَـتِ سَبْقاً

(And by the racers, racing.) It has been narrated from `Ali, Masruq, Mujahid, Abu Salih, and Al-Hasan Al-Basri that this means the angels.

فَالْمُدَبِّرَتِ أَمْراً

(And by those who arrange affairs.) `Ali, Mujahid, `Ata', Abu Salih, Al-Hasan, Qatadah, Ar-Rabi` bin Anas, and As-Suddi all said, "They are the angels." Al-Hasan added, "They control the affairs from the heaven to the earth, meaning by the command of their Lord, the Mighty and Majestic."

The Description of the Day of Judgement, the People, and what They will say

يَوْمَ تَرْجُفُ الرَّاجِفَةُ

(On the Day the Rajifah shakes.) Ibn `Abbas, Mujahid, Al-Hasan, Qatadah, Ad-Dahhak and others mentioned that this is the first blast of the Trumpet.

Mujahid reported that this is similar to Allahs statement:

يَوْمَ تَرْجُفُ الاٌّرْضُ وَالْجِبَالُ

(On the Day the earth and the mountains shake.) (73:14)

تَتْبَعُهَا الرَّادِفَةُ

(Followed by the Radifah.) Ibn `Abbas, Mujahid, Al-Hasan, Qatadah, Ad-Dahhak and others mentioned that this is the second blast of the Trumpet.

Mujahid reports that this is like Allah's statement:

وَحُمِلَتِ الاٌّرْضُ وَالْجِبَالُ فَدُكَّتَا دَكَّةً وَحِدَةً

(And the earth and mountains shall be removed from their places, and crushed with a single crushing.) (69:14)".

قُلُوبٌ يَوْمَئِذٍ وَاجِفَةٌ

(Hearts that Day will tremble.) Ibn `Abbas said, "This means afraid." Mujahid and Qatadah also said this.

أَبْصَـرُهَا خَـشِعَةٌ

(Their vision humiliated.) meaning, the eyes of the people. It means that the eyes will be lowly and disgraced from what they will witness of terrors.

يَقُولُونَ أَءِنَّا لَمَرْدُودُونَ فِى الْحَـفِرَةِ

(They say: "Shall we indeed be brought back from Al-Hafirah") meaning, the idolators of the Quraysh and whoever rejects the Hereafter as they did. They consider the occurrence of the resurrection after being placed in Al-Hafirah -- which are the graves -- as something farfetched. This has been said by Mujahid. They feel that this is something impossible after the destruction of their physical bodies and the disintegration of their bones and their decaying.

أَءِذَا كُنَّا عِظَـماً نَّخِرَةً

(Even after we are bones Nakhirah.) It has also been recited: (نَاخِرَةً) (Naakhirah). Ibn `Abbas, Mujahid and Qatadah, all said, "This means decayed." Ibn `Abbas said, "It is the bone when it has decayed and air enters into it."

قَالُوا۟ تِلْكَ إِذًۭا كَرَّةٌ خَاسِرَةٌۭ

(They say: "It would in that case be a return with loss.") Muhammad bin Ka`b said that the Quraysh said, "If Allah brings us back to life after we die, then surely we will be losers."

فَإِنَّمَا هِىَ زَجْرَةٌ وَحِدَةٌ

(But it will be only a single Zajrah.) meaning, this is a matter that is from Allah that will not occur twice, nor will there be any opportunity to affirm it or verify it.

زَجْرَةٌ: "a shout". Nominative feminine indefinite noun (اسم مرفوع).

This is similar/related to:

يَوْمَ يَدْعُوكُمْ فَتَسْتَجِيبُونَ بِحَمْدِهِ وَتَظُنُّونَ إِن لَّبِثْتُمْ إِلاَّ قَلِيلاً

(On the Day when He will call you, and you will answer with His praise and obedience, and you will think that you have stayed but a little while.)(17:52)

وَمَآ أَمْرُنَآ إِلاَّ وَحِدَةٌ كَلَمْحٍ بِالْبَصَرِ

(And our commandment is but one as the twinkling of an eye.) (54:50)

وَمَآ أَمْرُ السَّاعَةِ إِلاَّ كَلَمْحِ الْبَصَرِ أَوْ هُوَ أَقْرَبُ

(And the matter of the Hour is not but as a twinkling of the eye, or even nearer.) (16:77)

فَإِذَا هُم بِالسَّاهِرَةِ

(When behold, they are at As-Sahirah.) The people will be standing and looking. This will be when Allah commands the angel Israfil to blow into the Sur, which will be the blowing of the resurrection. At that time the first people and the last people will all be standing before their Lord looking.

Ibn `Abbas said, "As-Sahirah means the entire earth." Sa`id bin Jubayr, Qatadah and Abu Salih have all said this as well. `Ikrimah, Al-Hasan, Ad-Dahhak, and Ibn Zayd have all said, "As-Sahirah means the face of the earth." Mujahid said, "They will be at its (the earth's) lowest part, and they will be brought out to highest part." Then he said, "As-Sahirah is a level place."

السَّاهِرَة: a noun.

This is similar/related to:

يَوْمَ تُبَدَّلُ الاٌّرْضُ غَيْرَ الاٌّرْضِ وَالسَّمَـوَتُ وَبَرَزُواْ للَّهِ الْوَاحِدِ الْقَهَّارِ

(On the Day when the earth will be changed to another earth and so will be the heavens, and they will appear before Allah, the One, the Irresistible.) (14:48)

وَيَسْـَلُونَكَ عَنِ الْجِبَالِ فَقُلْ يَنسِفُهَا رَبِّى نَسْفاً - فَيَذَرُهَا قَاعاً صَفْصَفاً - لاَّ تَرَى فِيهَا عِوَجاً وَلا أَمْتاً

(And they ask you concerning the mountains: say, "My Lord will blast them and scatter them as particles of dust. Then He shall leave them as a level smooth plain.You will see therein nothing crooked or curved.) (20:105-107)

وَيَوْمَ نُسَيِّرُ الْجِبَالَ وَتَرَى الاٌّرْضَ بَارِزَةً

(And the Day We shall cause the mountains to pass away, and you will see the earth as a leveled plain.) (18:47)

هَلْ أَتَىٰكَ حَدِيثُ مُوسَىٰٓ إِذْ نَادَىٰهُ رَبُّهُۥ بِٱلْوَادِ ٱلْمُقَدَّسِ طُوًى

فَقُلْ هَل لَّكَ إِلَىٰٓ أَن تَزَكَّىٰ

وَأَهْدِيَكَ إِلَىٰ رَبِّكَ فَتَخْشَىٰ

إِنَّ فِى ذَٰلِكَ لَعِبْرَةًۭ لِّمَن يَخْشَىٰٓ

ءَأَنتُمْ أَشَدُّ خَلْقًا أَمِ ٱلسَّمَآءُ ۚ بَنَىٰهَا

رَفَعَ سَمْكَهَا فَسَوَّىٰهَا
